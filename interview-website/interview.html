Miranda Arsal

Flooreham Park, New Jersey
Lehigh University
December 23, 2000

Q: What is your major and your favorite part about it?

A: I am an IDEAS Major specializing in Computer Science and Design. I really like that I can explore a lot of different things and take all kinds of classes.

Q: Do you have any hobbies?

A: I like to write and journal. I also like to draw.

Q: Do you have any pets or siblings?

A: I don’t have any pets but do I have and older sister

Q: What is your favorite place to visit or the most interesting place you have been?

A:I really enjoy going to Cape Cod with my family, but the most interesting place I have been is when I went to Europe for a school trip and I went to Switzerland

Q: Do you like to read, if so what is your favorite type of book to read?

A: I do like to read. I really like drama or historical fiction books. I also like to read nonfiction books about things I am interested in. I really don’t like fantasy or science fiction books.

Q: When you were little, what did you want to be when you grew up?

A: When I was really little I wanted to be a writer and illustrator. Then when I was around eight years old I wanted to be a fashion designer/spy. (Fashion designer by day and spy by night)

Q: What was your favorite subject in school when you were younger?

A: I like reading and writing, so I guess language arts classes

Q: Do you have a favorite sport to play or watch?

A: I played softball and tennis in high school, and I like to watch soccer.

Q: If you were to go to school somewhere that is not Lehigh, where would you go?

A: University of Pennsylvania

Q: What is your biggest pet peeve?

A: Fake people.

Thanks for reading!
